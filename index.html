Decentralised Gpu Network

DGPU revolutionizes computational resource sharing with a truly decentralized GPU network, targeting the monopolistic challenges posed by centralized GPU vendors. By mobilizing idle GPUs from individuals globally, it democratizes access to vital computational power for AI and ML advancements. This innovative network supports sustainable development, reduces environmental impact, and fosters a token-based economy that rewards contributors.
